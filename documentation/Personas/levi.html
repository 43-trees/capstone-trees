Persona: Levi Davis, Urban Farmer

As an urban farmer, I want to reduce food waste and share my abundance with my community.

Technology

iPhone 11 with iOS 17. Surface Pro 7 with Windows 10. Chrome. Intermediate tech user

Love/Hate

  • Levi hates not being able to sell or donate all of his available produce before it overripens or spoils
  • He hates complex websites and social media sites that require a lot of promotion to maintain his audience
  • Levi loves being able to educate others about his farm and share his abundant crops when he can
  • Levi loves being able to connect with his community about the benefits of local foodways

Attitudes & Needs

  • Levi wants to reduce food waste in his community
  • He needs a way to share his crop excess with as little managing, negotiating, or direction as possible
  • Levi already shares much of his excess crops with his community through various nonprofits and volunteer networks but needs a way to get them directly and quickly into people’s hands

Use Case & Interaction Flow

Preconditions

Levi is signed into the site and has it pulled up on his phone

Post-conditions

Levi’s fruit trees are listed on the website so community can harvest his surplus crop

  • Levi clicks on the button to add a new tree to the map.
  • The site returns a pop-up with fields of information for Levi to complete.
  • Levi chooses “upload a photo” to add a picture of the tree he wants to list.
  • The site opens his photo library on his phone.
  • Levi selects the photo of the tree from his library.
  • The site returns to the pop-up form with the photo of the tree now visible in the photo field.
  • Levi selects the tree filter to add the tree type.
  • The site returns a drop-down menu with fruit tree options.
  • Levi selects "apple".
  • Levi types the address of the tree and adds in the “notes” section of the pop-up information about where to park on his farm. He clicks “save” at the bottom of the pop-up.
  • The site closes the pop-up and returns the posted pin on the map with all the information.
  • Levi exits the site knowing he’s able to share his excess fruit with anyone who finds it.
