Frank Sliver

Likes and Dislikes:

Frank is an enthusiastic and experienced food forager who has a passion for harvesting free and natural food resources, particularly from fruit trees. Frank enjoys spending time in nature, appreciating the beauty of the outdoors. Frank strongly dislikes the idea of food waste and actively seeks to reduce it through foraging. Frank values building a sense of community. Frank dislikes uninformed about local resources.

Technology

Uses and iPhone 11 Using iOS 17. Safari. No computer or tablets. Not up to date on the latest technology

Goals and Needs:

Frank's primary goal is to find an app that helps him locate and access free food resources, specifically fruit trees, in his local area. He is looking for an easy tool to identify locations, share information with other foragers, and reduce food waste. Frank needs an app that provides accurate and current information on the locations of fruit trees in his area, including details about the types of fruit available and the seasonality of each.

User Story:

As an experienced forager, I want to log on to my food harvest app to find local fruit trees easily.

Use Case

Frank the experienced food forager, woke up one sunny morning and noticed harvest season had started for the apple trees, Frank decided to log on to the website to check his local area for trees bearing fruit.

Preconditions

Frank wants to check if the fruit trees are ready for harvest on the app he is using.

Post-conditions

Frank finds a fruit tree that is ready to harvest and goes to harvest.

  • Frank takes out his iPhone, which he has already logged into the free food harvest website. He taps on the app icon.
  • The website's landing page loads on Frank's screen, displaying a map with icons representing various fruit tree locations in his vicinity.
  • Frank scrolls through the map to find the nearest trees ready for harvest.
  • The site asks if would you like to share your location.
  • Frank clicks yes and shares his location.
  • The website shows Frank the nearby trees.
  • Frank chooses one tree he visited last harvest season.
  • The website loads a detailed page about the selected tree, providing information such as the tree type, precise location, and availability status.
  • Frank scrolls through the details quickly and sees it hasn't changed since last season, but sees the icon for available its lit.
  • Frank noticed a new icon posted 2 days ago from the owner they are ready for harvest.
  • Frank gathers his foraging equipment and follows the directions on the website to reach the tree. He respectfully harvests the fruit and enjoys the fresh produce.
